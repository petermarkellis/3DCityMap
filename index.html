Taxi Taxi — a data experiment

A real-time 3D render of Manhattan, built from live OpenStreetMap geometry and layered with real NYC Open Data: yellow-taxi and Citi Bike trips, 311 complaints, vehicle collisions and crime reports. Everything is placed where it actually happened and tied to the hour it happened, so the city replays its own day as you scrub the time of day.
